    MandelbrotViewerQt

    Mandelbrot viewer with a minimalist UI and controls.

    Implemented as a Qt QWidget so you can include it in your own projects.

    • Left click = zoom in
    • Right click = zoom out
    • Left/Right arrows = change colors
